C Arun

C Arun is an Indian professional kabaddi player who plays for the Telugu Titans in the Pro Kabaddi League. He was born on 5th March 1993 and hails from the city of Thanjavur which is also known as the “Rice Bowl of Tamil Nadu”. He is a defender who primarily plays in the left cover role. He is disciplined in his defending, and is surprisingly quick and agile. He also brings the same discipline to his fitness regime and can be always counted upon during long stretch of matches. He is also good at anticipating the movement of the raider which often proves invaluable for the team.
