Siddharth Desai

Siddharth Sirish Desai is an Indian professional kabaddi player who plays for the Telugu Titans in the Pro Kabaddi League. He was born on 5th December 1991 in Hundalewadi which is a small town in Maharashtra. He plays as a raider and has performed very well for his team in the matches so far in this season of PKL. Having a towering presence and good raiding skills, he has already grabbed 28 points in two matches with super 10s in both the matches. He is coming off a surprise element for the defence of the teams on the other side of the mat. Apart from kabaddi he is a guitarist.

Here is a little more about me...

Making his VIVO Pro Kabaddi debut for U Mumba in Season 6, Siddharth Desai scored a Super 10 in his first match itself to announce his arrival on the big stage in style. In the same season, he went on to become the fastest player to reach 50 raid points at the time. His speed allows him to surprise defenders with the Running Hand Touch and catch them off-guard while his brute strength lets him push through the defence and return to his half, making him an extremely dangerous raider.
