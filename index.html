WE TITANS

Who are we

Telugu Titans is a kabaddi team based in Hyderabad. The team captain for PKL Season 8 is Rohit Kumar and the vice captain is Siddharth Sirish Desai. The head coach for Season 8 Mr. Jagadeesh Kumble. Telugu Titans is a franchise of Veera Sports owned by Mr. Srinivas Sreeramaneni of Vaya Group, Mr. Goutham Reddy Nedurmalli of NED Group and Mr. Mahesh Kolli of Greenko Group. The Titans play their home matches at Rajiv Gandhi Indoor Stadium, when in Vizag and at G. M. C. Balayogi SATS Indoor Stadium when in Hyderabad.
