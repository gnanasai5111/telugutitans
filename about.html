Know Us

Telugu Titans is a franchise of the Pro Kabaddi League. The team was founded in 2014 and has participated in the League since itâ€™s launch that same year. Season 2 of PKL (2015) saw us fight our way to the play offs and stand second in the League Table. This was also the year that Greenko stepped up to become our primary sponsor, and we decided to retain Rahul Chaudhari, marking the beginning of a long and rewarding relationship with the poster boy of Indian Kabaddi. We made it to the top 5 in Seasons 3 and 4 (2016) as well, but the trophy has eluded us thus far. We are now well into our 6th Season as a Pro Kabaddi team, and look forward to getting our first taste of the championship in 2018.

Our History

2014 Season 1

Telugu Titans is a kabaddi team based in Hyderabad and Vizag that plays in the Pro Kabaddi League.The team is owned by Veera Sports constituting Srini Sreeramaneni of Core Green Group, Mahesh Kolli of Greenko Group and Gautham Nedurumalli of NED Group.

2015 Season 2

Advanced to get a position in playoff & stood 2nd in the league table. Signs on Greenko as their sponsor. Retains Rahul Chaudhari.

2016 (Jan) Season 3

One of the top 5 teams in the ProKabaddi League standing. Retains Rahul Chaudhari.

2016 (Jun) Season 4

Advanced to get a position in playoff. Retains Rahul Chaudhari.

2017 Season 5

Continues with Greenko as the sponsor. Retains Rahul Chaudhari.

2018 Season 6

Jagmohan signed on as Head Coach. Linn Caleb Seth joins as Fitness Trainer. Rahul Chaudhari retained.

2019 Season 7

Brought Siddharth Sirish Desai for 1.45Cr.
